GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM4514 is an advanced high voltage CMOS RF 
antenna switch driver in an ultra-small footprint. 
The SGM4514 supports 16V to 70V power supply 
voltage range and there is no damage if VCC plus VSPIKE 
is less than 77V (MAX), the transient spike voltage is 
generated by T/R switching. 
The SGM4514 is available in Green TQFN-4×4-16AL, 
TDFN-3×3-8BL and MSOP-8 (Exposed Pad) packages 
and operates over an operating temperature range of 
-40℃ to +125℃. 
FEATURES 
● High Speed and Low Power 
● Wide Output VCC Range: 16V to 70V 
● Low RDSON: 
 NMOS = 2Ω (TYP) 
 PMOS = 10Ω (TYP) 
● Input Frequency: 2.1MHz (TYP) 
● Low Static Current: ICC = 250μA 
● Low Dynamic Current at 100kHz: 
ICC_DYN = 0.65mA (TYP) 
● Available in Green TQFN-4×4-16AL, TDFN-3×3-8BL 
and MSOP-8 (Exposed Pad) Packages

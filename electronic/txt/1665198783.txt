GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM4056 is a low cost battery charger with 
integrated high input voltage capability for single-cell 
Li-Ion or Li-polymer batteries. The SGM4056 has a 
CC/CV charge profile required for Li-Ion battery. The 
SGM4056 features the over-voltage protection (OVP) 
function. The OVP threshold is typically 6.8V 
(SGM4056-6.8) 
or 
10.5V 
(SGM4056-10.5). 
The 
SGM4056 accepts a 26.5V maximum voltage for power 
input, when VIN > VOVP, the charger is disabled. 
The SGM4056 has a charge indication feature. When 
the charger is disabled or the input is floating, the 
leakage current from the battery is < 1μA. 
The SGM4056 is available in Green TDFN-3×3-8L, 
TDFN-2×3-8L, TDFN-2×2-8L and SOIC-8 (Exposed 
Pad) packages and is rated over the -40℃ to +85℃ 
temperature range. 
 
FEATURES 
● Input Over-Voltage Protection Thresholds: 
 6.8V (TYP) for SGM4056-6.8 
 10.5V (TYP) for SGM4056-10.5 
● Trickle Charge Threshold: 2.55V 
● Power Input Voltage up to 26.5V 
● Less Components and Low Cost 
● Selectable Charge Current 
● Selectable EOC Current 
● Thermal Regulation Function 
● Charging Status Indication 
● 1μA (MAX) Leakage Current when Charger 
Disabled or Input Floating 
● Available in Green TDFN-3×3-8L, TDFN-2×3-8L, 
TDFN-2×2-8L and SOIC-8 (Exposed Pad) Packages

Handheld Devices 
Smart Phones

Standalone Chargers

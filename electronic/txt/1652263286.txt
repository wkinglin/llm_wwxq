GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM3005 is a dual bidirectional SPDT (single- 
pole/double-throw), 
TTL/CMOS 
compatible 
analog 
switch. It operates from a 1.8V to 5.5V single power 
supply. 
The SGM3005 features low on-resistance, low voltage 
and fast switching times. The high performances make it 
very suitable for multiple applications, such as portable 
equipment, audio and video signal routing, etc. In 
addition, the SGM3005 can be used as a dual 2-to-1 
multiplexer because it has two normally open and two 
normally close switches. Low power consumption is 
also one of the important reasons that make it a good 
choice. 
The SGM3005 is available in Green TDFN-3×3-10L 
and MSOP-10 packages. It operates over an ambient 
temperature range of -40℃ to +125℃. 
 
FEATURES 
● Single Supply Voltage Range: 1.8V to 5.5V 
● -3dB Bandwidth: 15MHz 
● Ultra Low On-Resistance: 0.5Ω (TYP) 
● Low On-Resistance Flatness 
● Low Crosstalk: -90dB (TYP) at 100kHz 
● Fast Switching Times (V+ = 5V): 
tON: 50ns 
tOFF: 15ns 
● Off-Isolation: -69dB at 100kHz 
● Low Power Consumption 
● Rail-to-Rail Input and Output Operation 
● TTL/CMOS Compatible 
● -40℃ to +125℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in Green TDFN-3×3-10L and MSOP-10 
Packages

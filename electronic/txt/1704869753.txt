GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGMPM21330 is a power MOSFET with a low 
on-state resistance and low gate charge. This feature 
makes it a good choice for a load switch application. 
 
FEATURES 
● Low On-State Resistance 
● High-Speed Switching 
● Halogen-Free/RoHS Compliant 
 
APPLICATIONS 
Load Switch Applications 
High-Speed Line Driver 
Relay Driver Applications 
Handheld and Mobile Applications 
USB Connector VBUS Power Switch

GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM4782 is a TTL/CMOS compatible analog 
multiplexer which consists of two 4-channel multiplexers. 
It operates from 1.8V to 4.2V single power supply that 
can handle rail-to-rail analog signals. 
The SGM4782 features low voltage, low on-resistance 
and fast switching times. The high performances make 
it very suitable for multiple applications, such as cellular 
phones, audio and video signal routing, etc. 
The SGM4782 can and is available in Green 
TQFN-3×3-16L and TSSOP-16 packages. It operates 
over an ambient temperature range of -40℃ to +85℃. 
 
 
FEATURES 
● Single Supply Voltage Range: 1.8V to 4.2V 
● Low On-Resistance: 0.5Ω (TYP) at 4.2V 
● Low On-Resistance Flatness 
● High Off-Isolation: -55dB (RL = 50Ω, f = 1MHz) 
● -3dB Bandwidth: 30MHz 
● Fast Switching Times (V+ = 4.2V) 
tON: 20ns 
tOFF: 20ns 
● Low Crosstalk: -108dB (RL = 50Ω, f = 1MHz) 
● Low Power Consumption 
● Break-Before-Make Switching 
● TTL/CMOS Compatible 
● Rail-to-Rail Input and Output Operation 
● -40℃ to +85℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in Green TQFN-3×3-16L and TSSOP-16 
Packages

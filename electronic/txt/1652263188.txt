GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM2268 is a dual SPDT (single-pole/double-throw) 
analog switch. It operates from a 1.8V to 4.2V single 
power supply. 
The SGM2268 features ultra-low on-resistance, low 
voltage and fast switching times. The high performances 
make it very suitable for multiple applications, such as 
portable equipment, audio and video signal routing, etc. 
In addition, the SGM2268 can be used as a dual 2-to-1 
multiplexer because it has two normally open and two 
normally close switches. Low power consumption is 
also one of the important reasons that make it a good 
choice. 
The SGM2268 is available in a Green TQFN-1.8×1.4-10L 
package. It operates over an ambient temperature 
range of -40℃ to +85℃. 
 
FEATURES 
● Single Supply Voltage Range: 1.8V to 4.2V 
● Ultra Low On-Resistance: 0.4Ω (TYP) at 4.2V 
● Low On-Resistance Flatness: 0.08Ω (TYP) 
● Low On-Resistance Matching : 0.04Ω (TYP) 
● -3dB Bandwidth: 40MHz 
● Fast Switching Times at V+ = 4.2V: 
tON: 88ns 
tOFF: 16ns 
● High Off-Isolation: -78dB at 100kHz 
● Low Crosstalk: -103dB at 100kHz 
● Break-Before-Make Switching 
● TTL/CMOS Compatible 
● Rail-to-Rail Input and Output Operation 
● -40℃ to +85℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in a Green TQFN-1.8×1.4-10L Package

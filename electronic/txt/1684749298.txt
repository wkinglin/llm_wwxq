GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM9155 is a 1-channel, 6th-order output 
reconstruction filter which can operate from 3.1V to 
5.5V single power supply. It is designed to replace 
passive LC filters and drivers with an integrated device. 
One channel is High Definition (HD) filter. 
The device allows DC- or AC-coupled output. SGM9155 
can be DC-coupled or AC-coupled with input video 
signal to eliminate out-of-band noise, such as the 
output stage of DAC. Internal bias circuitry may be 
used for providing constant bias voltage if AC-coupled 
inputs are required. 
The SGM9155 is available in Green SOT-23-6 and 
SC70-5 packages. It operates over an ambient 
temperature range of -40℃ to +85℃. 
 
FEATURES 
● Supply Voltage Range: 3.1V to 5.5V 
● One 6th-Order 720p High Definition Filter 
● Bias Mode Active with AC-Coupled Inputs 
● Bias Mode Inactive with DC-Coupled Inputs 
● AC- or DC-Coupled Output 
● DC-Coupled Outputs Eliminate AC-Coupled  
Capacitors 
● -40℃ to +85℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in Green SOT-23-6 and SC70-5 Packages

GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM4684 is a dual, SPDT (single-pole/double-throw) 
CMOS analog switch. It operates from a 1.8V to 5.5V 
single power supply. 
The SGM4684 features ultra-low on-resistance, low 
voltage and fast switching times. The high performances 
make it very suitable for multiple applications, such as 
portable equipment, audio and video signal routing, etc. 
In addition, the SGM4684 can be used as a dual 2-to-1 
multiplexer because it has two normally open and two 
normally close switches. Low power consumption is 
also one of the important reasons that make it a good 
choice. 
The SGM4684 is available in a Green WLCSP-2.0×1.5-10B 
package. It operates over an ambient temperature 
range of -40℃ to +125℃. 
 
 
FEATURES 
● Single Supply Voltage Range: 1.8V to 5.5V 
● Ultra Low On-Resistance: 0.4Ω (TYP) at 5V 
● Low On-Resistance Flatness: 0.1Ω (TYP) 
● Low On-Resistance Matching: 0.04Ω (TYP) 
● -3dB Bandwidth: 13MHz 
● Fast Switching Times at V+ = 5V: 
tON: 25ns 
tOFF: 28ns 
● High Off-Isolation: -70dB at 100kHz 
● Low Crosstalk: -100dB at 100kHz 
● Low Power Consumption 
● Break-Before-Make Switching 
● TTL/CMOS Compatible 
● Rail-to-Rail Input and Output Operation 
● -40℃ to +125℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in a Green WLCSP-2.0×1.5-10B Package

GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM4806 is a 2:1 MUX input, audio click-pop 
eliminator which can operate from a 2.7V to 12V single 
power supply. It is designed for HiFi audio system or 
portable devices. 
The SGM4806 allows -VCC to +VCC wide range audio 
signals passing with low distortion. An external 
processor can eliminate the click-pop noise. 
The SGM4806 is available in Green TQFN-4×4-16L, 
WLCSP-1.27×2.13-15B and SOIC-16 packages. It 
operates over an ambient temperature range of -40℃ 
to +85℃. 
FEATURES 
● Supply Voltage Range: 2.7V to 12V 
● 2:1 MUX Input 
● Low On-Resistance: 0.8Ω (TYP) 
● -VCC to +VCC Rail-to-Rail Low Distortion Audio 
Signals Passing 
● High Off-Isolation 
● Low Crosstalk 
● Low THD+N 
● 1.8V Logic Control 
● -40℃ to +85℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in Green WLCSP-1.27×2.13-15B, 
SOIC-16 and TQFN-4×4-16L Packages 
 
 
 
APPLICATIONS 
HiFi Audio System 
Portable Equipment

GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGMNQ28430 is a power MOSFET with a low 
on-state resistance.  
 
 
 
FEATURES 
● Low On-State Resistance 
● Low Total Gate Charge and Capacitance Losses 
● Small Footprint (5×6 mm2) for Compact Design 
● RoHS Compliant and Halogen Free 
 
APPLICATIONS 
CPU Power Delivery 
DC/DC Converters 
Power Load Switch 
Notebook Battery Management

GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM3749 is a high efficiency white LED driver with 
a 1.25MHz boost converter. With the fixed switching 
frequency and an internal 40V/1.5A switch FET, the 
SGM3749 is designed for powering high brightness 
LEDs in general lighting. 
The SGM3749 is capable of driving up to 10 white 
LEDs in series. The FB feedback voltage is regulated at 
200mV 
typically. 
The 
default 
LED 
current 
is 
programmed by an external RSET resistor. During the 
operation, the LED current can be controlled by 
applying a PWM signal to the CTRL pin. The feedback 
voltage depends on the PWM signal duty cycle. For 
PWM dimming control, there are no audible noises on 
the output capacitor. 
The SGM3749 integrates LED open protection. It 
prevents the device from damaging due to the over- 
voltage during LED open conditions. 
The SGM3749 is available in Green TDFN-2×2-6L and 
TSOT-23-6 packages. It operates over an ambient 
temperature range of -40℃ to +85℃. 
FEATURES 
● Input Voltage Range: 3V to 20V 
● Integrated 40V/1.5A Switch 
● Up to 90% Efficiency 
● Switching Frequency: 1.25MHz 
● Feedback Voltage: 200mV 
● PWM Dimming Control 
● PWM Dimming Frequency: 5kHz to 100kHz 
● 38V LED Open Protection for 10 LEDs 
● 600kΩ Pull-Down Resistor on CTRL Pin 
● Dimming Stable in More than 1:500 PWM Range 
● Automatic Soft-Start for Reducing Inrush Current 
● -40℃ to +85℃ Operating Temperature Range 
● Available in Green TDFN-2×2-6L and TSOT-23-6

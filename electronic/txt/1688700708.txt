GENERAL DESCRIPTION 
The SGM835 has a wide input common-mode voltage 
range from -0.3V to 72V, and is available with gains of 
12.5V/V, 20V/V and 50V/V. The bandwidth of 
small-signal can reach up to 160kHz. 
The SGM835 is available in a Green MSOP-8 package. It 
is specified over a junction temperature range of -40℃ 
to +125℃.

● Gain: 12.5V/V, 20V/V or 50V/V 
● Low Gain Error: 0.2% (MAX)
